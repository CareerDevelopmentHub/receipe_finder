Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan, add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  4. Add tomatoes, biryani masala, and cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  5. Stir in yogurt, coriander, mint leaves, and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add rice and water. Season with salt.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is cooked and the chicken is tender.

Serving:

Chicken biryani can be served hot with raita and salad.
