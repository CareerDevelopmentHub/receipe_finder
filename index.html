Biriyani

Biryani, biriani, beryani or beriani is an Indian dish that comes in set of rice-based foods made with spices, rice (usually basmati)

Dosa

A dosa is a thin savory crepe in South Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground black lentils and rice.

Butter Chicken
